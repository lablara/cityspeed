CitySpeed

This application is part of the masters project of the postgraduate program in applied computing of the State University of Feira de Santana (UEFS).

The velocity collection is performed in such a way that the data does not have a user identification, obtaining the approximate instantaneous velocity taking into account the variation of the geographic position in a certain interval of time..

The data collected in this application are sent to the Internet to carry out analysis of vehicle speeds in urban areas.
